Contact/About Us

We are dedicated to creating innovative designs that merge minimalism with modern streetwear aesthetics. Our mission is to provide quality pieces that speak to the contemporary individual.

All of our items are handmade with care and attention to detail, ensuring both style and comfort for our customers. Each piece is crafted locally, making every garment unique and special.

We offer custom orders tailored to your preferences! Whether you want a specific design, color scheme, or personalized touch, we can work with you to create a one-of-a-kind piece that reflects your individual style. Reach out to us to discuss your custom order ideas.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to Purchase or Inquire About Any Item?

Please reach out to @reh.creation on Instagram or any of the other socials provided below. We're happy to answer any questions and help you with your order!

What is the return policy?

All items are final sale, we do not offer returns. In the event of receiving a faulty product or incorrect size, please contact customer service using the information below.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping is dependent on your location and the items in your order. You will always know the shipping price before you purchase.

When will I get my order?

We will work quickly to ship your order as soon as possible. Orders will be shipped between 10 - 14 business days.

Defects

Because each hoodie is handmade using woven tapestry fabric there might be slight imperfections. These can include minor weaving inconsistencies, small thread pulls, minimal size variations, or light asymmetry in the design. In some cases, there may also be very small rips or loose areas in the weave. These characteristics are a natural result of artisanal craftsmanship and fabric texture, making each piece one of a kind.
